# UI Rules

## Nguyen tac tong quat
- Moi man hinh chi co 1 muc tieu chinh.
- Uu tien workflow thay vi dump du lieu.
- Khong hien thi hai khoi trung nghia nghiep vu.

## Lenh san xuat
- Header ngan, CTA ro rang.
- Co overview metric nho.
- Danh sach LSX la khu trung tam.
- Panel chi tiet chi giu thong tin phuc vu quyet dinh.

## NK NVL
- Khu "Hang cho cap nhat NVL" phai dung de bat dau viec.
- Bang duoi la lich su / giao dich da phat sinh.
- Khong lap lai cung mot LSX qua nhieu khoi neu khong can.

## Detail panel
- An cac field rong neu khong can de quyet dinh.
- Nhom thong tin theo y nghia: tong quan, ke hoach, nhan su, tien do.

## Visual hierarchy
- Mau chi de the hien trang thai.
- Khong dung qua nhieu card lon rong.
- Bang dung cho danh sach, card dung cho tong quan, drawer dung cho form.
