# Form Rules

## Tong quat
- Moi field phai co label ro rang.
- Field bat buoc can co validate ro.
- Group field theo section nghiep vu.

## Form LSX
- Chi nhap thong tin dau don.
- Khong hien thi field thuoc giao dich NVL thuc te.

## Form NK NVL
- Phai chia section:
  1. Thong tin LSX
  2. Chung tu
  3. Cong doan / tho
  4. Trong luong
  5. Tinh hao / NXT

## Rule mo lai form
- Mo lai cung ma LSX thi form phai lay du lieu gan nhat.
- Khong reset field da nhap neu user dang tiep tuc cap nhat.
