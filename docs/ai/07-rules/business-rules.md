# Business Rules

## Rule 1: LSX va NK NVL la hai lop du lieu khac nhau
- LSX chua thong tin dau don.
- NK NVL chua phat sinh thuc te.
- Khong duoc tron vai tro hai lop nay.

## Rule 2: Uu tien nguon du lieu khi mo form NK NVL
Khi user mo form theo ma LSX, he thong phai nap theo thu tu:
1. Draft chua luu cua chinh LSX do
2. Giao dich NVL gan nhat cua chinh LSX do
3. Header goc cua LSX

## Rule 3: Khong reset du lieu da nhap
- Neu user da nhap du lieu cho LSX, he thong khong duoc mo lai form trang.
- Mau xanh highlight khong duoc xem la thay the cho co che luu.

## Rule 4: Chot LSX
- Khong cho them giao dich moi
- Khong cho xoa giao dich
- Khong cho sua status van hanh neu da chot

## Rule 5: Pending LSX
- Pending chi co nghia la chua co giao dich NVL dau tien.
- Neu da co movement hoac da co draft dang dung, khong duoc xem nhu moi hoan toan.

## Rule 6: Bao cao
- Ton hop tho va hao hut phai tong hop tu movement.
- Khong tong hop truc tiep tu header LSX.
